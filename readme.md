# **🔴 PHASE 1 — Months 1–3: Build Your Foundation**

## **Month 1**

- Buy IELTS preparation book — Cambridge IELTS 15, 16, 17
- Start Duolingo German daily (minimum 20 minutes)
- Watch YouTube — 'Deutsch für Euch' and 'Learn German with Jenny'
- Research Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship (Hungary — fully funded)
- Research Polish universities — Warsaw University of Technology, AGH University
- Maintain your semester studies — don't let CGPA drop

## **Month 2**

- Focus heavily on IELTS Reading & Listening (easier to improve fast)
- Join a free or affordable IELTS coaching in Dhaka or online
- Complete German A1 vocabulary list (800–1000 basic words)
- Open a dedicated savings account — start saving even small amounts
- Make a list of 10 target universities in Europe

## **Month 3**

- Take a full IELTS mock test at home — check your current score
- Identify your weak areas (Speaking? Writing?)
- Enroll at Goethe Institut Dhaka for formal German A1 course
- Start building your CV — add your CGPA, projects, any activities
- Ask a teacher at CUB to be your recommendation letter provider later

# **🟡 PHASE 2 — Months 4–6: Build Your Scores & Skills**

## **Month 4**

- Intensify IELTS Speaking & Writing practice
- Practice speaking English every single day — record yourself
- Complete German A1 course and get certificate
- Start German A2 self-study
- Research scholarship deadlines — Stipendium Hungaricum deadline is usually January–February

## **Month 5**

- Book your IELTS exam — give it this month or Month 6
- Do minimum 3 full mock tests before the real exam
- Shortlist universities to 5–6 final choices
- Check each university's anabin recognition on anabin.kmk.org
- Start writing a draft Statement of Purpose (SOP)

## **Month 6**

- Give IELTS exam — Target 6.0 minimum, 6.5 is ideal
- If score is low — don't panic, you can retake in Month 8
- Complete German A2
- Finalize your SOP first draft
- Discuss with your family about financial planning for visa

# **🟢 PHASE 3 — Months 7–9: Apply Everywhere**

## **Month 7**

**Collect all documents needed for applications:**

- SSC & HSC certificates + transcripts
- CUB semester transcripts
- IELTS result
- Passport (make sure it's valid 5+ years)
- Recommendation letters (2 minimum)
- SOP finalized
- Start German B1 preparation
- Apply to Stipendium Hungaricum if deadline is open (check each year — usually Nov–Feb)

## **Month 8**

- Submit university applications to your shortlisted schools
- Apply to Poland, Hungary, Czech Republic universities
- If IELTS score was low — retake exam this month
- Continue German B1 study
- Research part-time job rules in your target country

## **Month 9**

- Follow up on application statuses
- If you get conditional offers — fulfill requirements quickly
- Start preparing for visa interview (common questions, required docs)
- Research cost of living in your target city (Warsaw, Budapest, Prague etc.)
- Connect with Bangladeshi student communities in your target country — Facebook groups are helpful

# **🔵 PHASE 4 — Months 10–12: Get Ready to Fly**

## **Month 10**

- Receive admission letter — Congratulations if you're here!
- Start student visa application immediately — it takes 6–12 weeks
- Arrange financial documents — bank statement showing required funds
- Get medical insurance sorted
- Reach German B1 level — keep going!

## **Month 11**

- Attend visa interview at embassy
- Arrange accommodation in target city (university dorm is cheapest)
- Book flight tickets early for cheaper price
- Connect with students already there — ask about arrival tips
- Learn basic life skills — cooking, budgeting, independent living

## **Month 12**

- Receive your visa
- Prepare your luggage and necessary items
- Inform CUB about your situation (you may take a break or transfer credits)
- Say goodbyes and fly to your new life!
